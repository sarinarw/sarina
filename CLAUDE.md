## Affiliate links

Affiliate links are marked with `*` in the link text, e.g. `[Buy on Amazon*](https://amzn.to/...)`.

Any post that contains an affiliate link must include this disclosure at the top of the post body (after the frontmatter):

```
*Links marked with \* are affiliate links — I may earn a small commission if you purchase through them, at no extra cost to you.*
```

Non-affiliate links (e.g. PSN store) do not need the disclosure and do not get a `*`.
